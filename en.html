Who am I ?

So you can get to know me.

A man with design in his heart

I have been curious about audiovisual media, graphic design, and content creation since I was 10 years old.

This passion motivated me to start my freelance business in 2021 in order to launch my YouTube channel and offer video editing services. These have been rewarding years during which I have been able to understand the codes of social media and work for a wide variety of clients, allowing me to learn new skills in design and video editing every day.

This year, having explored web design and website development, I would like to work on a work-study basis, contributing to your activities!

A technology enthusiast

Beyond these skills, I am self-taught and curious about everything related to technology :

- Continuous software improvement
- Artificial intelligence
- UI and UX Design
- Digital creation software

I also have experience in communication, particularly on social media, with my clients' videos and my personal channel with 100,000 followers on TikTok.

As a side note, I created this website entirely on my own, from the design to the development.
